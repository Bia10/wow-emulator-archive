How looting works

|0.0111|<-array pointer   	minrange=0.0111
maxrange=0.0111		  	|0.0134|<-array pointer

|0.4124|		  	|0.4124| 
|12.231|  		  	|12.231| if random number < maxrange  ->  take this loot
|24.341|		  	|24.341| and random number > minrange
